About Our School

Welcome to Viniyaka Vidya Mandir

Established in 2025, Viniyaka Vidya Mandir has been at the forefront of providing quality education that combines academic excellence with character development.

Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and ethical values to prepare students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for developing innovative, compassionate, and globally-minded citizens who contribute positively to society.
